Playstation 4 Controller (name: "PS4 Controller") Linux/MacOS

In pygame 2 the PS4 controller mapping has 6 Axes, 13 buttons and 1 Hat.

Left Stick:
Left -> Right   - Axis 0
Up   -> Down    - Axis 1

Right Stick:
Left -> Right   - Axis 3
Up   -> Down    - Axis 4

Left Trigger:
Out -> In       - Axis 2

Right Trigger:
Out -> In       - Axis 5

Buttons:
Cross Button    - Button 0
Circle Button   - Button 1
Triangle Button - Button 2
Square Button   - Button 3
Left Bumper     - Button 4
Right Bumper    - Button 5
Left Trigger    - Button 6
Right Trigger   - Button 7
Share Button    - Button 8
Options Button  - Button 9
PS Button       - Button 10
L. Stick In     - Button 11
R. Stick In     - Button 12

Hat:
D-pad Up        - (0, 1)
D-pad Down      - (0, -1)
D-pad Left      - (-1, 0)
D-pad Right     - (1, 0)
